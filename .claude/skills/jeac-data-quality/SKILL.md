# JEAC Data Quality

Use before market, technical, news, or portfolio analysis. State symbol, market, timezone, and as-of time. Prefer TWSE/TPEx/MOPS and issuer disclosures for Taiwan; issuer IR, SEC, and exchange sources for US securities. Label fallback sources.

Classify evidence as `verified`, `partial`, or `blocked`. A missing, stale, malformed, or contradictory required field is unavailable—not zero, bearish, or a forced sell. For each conclusion, expose source, as-of time, and limitation. Omit unsupported sections rather than repeating placeholders.
