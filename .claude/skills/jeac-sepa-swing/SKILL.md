# JEAC SEPA Swing

For Taiwan/US swing research, validate adjusted OHLCV before assessing market regime, Stage 2, SEPA, VCP, and pivot volume confirmation. A valid plan needs a conditional entry, structural stop, objective, invalidation, and at least 2:1 reward-to-risk. Keep unavailable evidence distinct from failed evidence.

Return `可觀察`, `等待確認`, `符合條件`, or `不符合`. Use 0.5–1.5% portfolio risk per idea only as a default. Do not average down, widen stops, make unsupported pattern claims, or execute orders.
