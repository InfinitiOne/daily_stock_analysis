# JEAC Portfolio Risk

Review position weight, loss to structural stop, total open risk, issuer/sector/country concentration, correlation, liquidity, FX, and market regime before changing a portfolio. Require holdings, quantities, prices/as-of time, and stops for numeric conclusions.

Prioritize `維持`, `降低風險`, `等待確認`, or `不新增` with explicit conditions. Never invent missing prices or stops, average down, widen a stop, or place orders.
