# JEAC News Intelligence

Use this skill for current Taiwan or US equity catalysts and risks. News supports a SEPA decision but never independently creates a trade recommendation.

## Evidence workflow

1. State symbol, market, cutoff time, lookback window, and next scheduled event. Prioritize MOPS/company releases for Taiwan and issuer IR/SEC for the US; use reputable reporting only as corroboration.
2. For every item record publication time, source URL, event type, fact versus management guidance versus commentary, and affected period. De-duplicate syndicated coverage.
3. Classify the transmission path only when evidence supports it: earnings/guidance, demand/orders, product/technology, margin/cost, regulation, capital raising/dilution, governance, industry/macro, litigation, or operations.
4. Connect material evidence to the SEPA checklist: earnings/sales acceleration, leadership/RS context, catalyst quality, and event risk near a pivot. Do not assign sentiment from headlines alone.

## Output contract

Provide a dated table of verified catalysts and risks, then state: confirmed facts, unverified claims, next event, and whether the evidence strengthens, weakens, or does not change the technical setup. If no verified recent item exists, say so once and omit empty sentiment and earnings sections. Never fabricate a news item, sentiment score, earnings expectation, or deadline.
