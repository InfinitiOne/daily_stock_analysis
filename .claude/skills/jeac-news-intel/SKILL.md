# JEAC News Intelligence

Collect dated, de-duplicated evidence and prioritize MOPS/company releases for Taiwan and issuer IR/SEC/exchange disclosures for US securities. Classify items as earnings/guidance, demand, product, regulation, financing/dilution, governance, macro, or operational risk.

For every item, provide date, source, verified claim, and a clearly labelled possible transmission path. Separate confirmed facts from rumor/commentary. If verified recent evidence is absent, say so once and omit empty sentiment/news placeholders. News alone never creates a trade recommendation.
